# Code of Conduct

Voice Watch follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and user safety.
- Avoid harassment, insults, and personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that
violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in a ban from the
project.
