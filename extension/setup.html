Connect Voice Watch

Follow these steps once for the unpacked browser extension.

1

Open your browser extensions page

Use Chrome or Edge, then turn on Developer mode in the top corner.

Chrome: chrome://extensions
Edge: edge://extensions
2

Load the Voice Watch extension

Click Load unpacked, then select this exact folder.
